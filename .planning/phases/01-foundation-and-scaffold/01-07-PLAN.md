Wire build-time env var validation (`@t3-oss/env-nextjs` with Zod schemas split into server-only and client-safe halves) AND ship the four agent-accessibility endpoints (`/llms.txt`, `/.well-known/mcp.json`, `/.well-known/openapi.yaml`, `/api/mcp/[transport]`) as real route handlers — not 404 stubs. FOUND-12 + agent-first design constraint (PROJECT.md): agent-accessibility stubs cost almost nothing at scaffold time and are 10x more expensive to retrofit (Pitfall 27). Shipping real responses on Phase 1 deploy proves to an agent crawler that this site speaks its protocol.
